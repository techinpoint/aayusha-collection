About Aayusha Collection

Aayusha Collection was founded in 2025 by Aayushma Ghimire, a passionate young entrepreneur who started her journey just after completing Grade 12.

Driven by a desire to provide high-quality, fashionable clothing, Aayusha Collection combines modern, traditional, and youth-focused styles to meet every trend with elegance.

Our goal is simple: to deliver the best-quality clothes and ensure customer satisfaction.

Unlike many brands that offer low-quality or reused clothes, we take pride in sourcing new, premium fabrics that elevate our customers' wardrobes.

Currently, we deliver across Kathmandu Valley and even send orders via local buses to customers outside the valley.

Follow us on TikTok for style tips and product showcases.
